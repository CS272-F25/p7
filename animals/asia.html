Explore Animals

Animals of Asia

Bengal Tiger

The iconic tiger of Asia, found in India and Southeast Asia, known for its striking orange coat with black stripes.

Giant Panda

Native to China, pandas are beloved for their distinctive black-and-white fur and bamboo diet.

Orangutan

Intelligent primates living in the rainforests of Borneo and Sumatra, with long reddish hair.

Asian Elephant

Smaller than African elephants, these gentle giants roam forests and grasslands across Asia.

Snow Leopard

Elusive mountain cats adapted to cold, rocky ranges of Central and South Asia.

Red Panda

Small, tree-dwelling mammals with reddish fur, found in the Himalayas and southwestern China.
